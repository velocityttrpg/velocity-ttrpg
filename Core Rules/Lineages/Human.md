# Human

Humanity's defining quality is not strength, magic, or longevity — it's potential. Humans learn faster and adapt more readily than any other people. In a world shaped by the supernatural, their mundane nature is both a vulnerability and a kind of camouflage.

**Size:** Medium
**Base Movement Speed:** 30 ft

## Traits

### Human Potential
Humans begin play with 12 Skill Points instead of 10.

### Mundane Presence
Humans carry no supernatural signature and cannot be detected by Supernatural Detection skills or similar abilities.

### Eye for Value
Humans have an instinctive sense for what things are worth — and what they can get for them. They notice valuables others overlook and extract more from every transaction.

### Uncanny Adaptability
Once per session, a Human may re-roll any failed Opposition Check and take the better result.

## Flaws

### Mortal Nature
Humans are more susceptible to supernatural compulsion and influence. −2 on Opposition Checks to resist mind-affecting supernatural abilities.

## Opposition Check Modifiers

+2 on Investigation Opposition Checks when searching for or assessing valuables.
+2 on Negotiation Opposition Checks when trading or establishing prices.

## Languages

Common.
