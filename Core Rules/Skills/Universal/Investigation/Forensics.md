# Forensics

**Branch:** Investigation  
**Tree:** Universal  
**Prerequisites:** Investigation I

## Description

Forensics is the discipline of reading physical evidence — crime scenes, bodies, residue, and wreckage — to reconstruct what happened and who was responsible. A forensics-trained character doesn't just observe a scene; they interrogate it. At mastery, they can walk into a room and read its history from the marks left behind.

## Training Tier

**Cost:** 1 SP

### Forensics IV

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Forensics III  
**Effect:** After examining a scene, the character may ask the Narrator whether supernatural forces were involved. The Narrator must answer yes or no.

## Mastery Tier

### Forensics VI

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Forensics V  
**Effect:** Once per session, after examining a scene for one minute, the character may ask the Narrator to describe the sequence of events that occurred there. The Narrator must provide an accurate account of what happened.
