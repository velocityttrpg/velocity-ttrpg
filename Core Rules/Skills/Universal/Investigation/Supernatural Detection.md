# Supernatural Detection

**Branch:** Investigation  
**Tree:** Universal  
**Prerequisites:** Investigation I

## Description

Supernatural Detection is the trained sensitivity to forces and entities that exist outside ordinary perception — the lingering residue of magic, the presence of non-human beings, the weight of the unnatural in a room. Most people feel unease and can't explain why. A character trained in Supernatural Detection knows exactly why. At mastery, they can identify what they're sensing and read its nature in detail.
