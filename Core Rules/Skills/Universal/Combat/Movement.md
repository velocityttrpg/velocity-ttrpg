# Movement

**Tree:** Universal  
**Prerequisites:** None

## Description

Movement represents a character's physical conditioning, spatial awareness, and combat mobility — the difference between a fighter who plants their feet and one who uses the entire battlefield. Investment in the Movement branch increases ground covered per AP, improves the character's ability to defend while in motion, and ultimately removes the threat of attacks of opportunity entirely. A character who invests here is harder to pin down and harder to hit when they move.

## Notes

1. Movement has no mastery tier. The training tier represents the full extent of purchasable Movement improvement.

## Training Tier

### Movement I

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** None  
**Effect:** +5 feet of movement per AP spent on movement.

### Movement II

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Movement I  
**Effect:** +1 to Dodge, Block, and Parry Opposition Checks.

### Movement III

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Movement II  
**Effect:** +1d10 to defense rolls against attacks of opportunity.

### Movement IV

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Movement III  
**Effect:** +5 feet of movement per AP spent when swimming, climbing, or crawling.

### Movement V

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Movement IV  
**Effect:** The character does not provoke reactions or attacks of opportunity when moving during combat.
