# Melee Attack

**Branch:** Combat  
**Tree:** Universal  
**Prerequisites:** None

## Description

Melee Attack represents a character's training in close-quarters combat. Skills in this line unlock weapon size categories, improve attack rolls and damage rolls with melee weapons, and grant additional AP at higher tiers. A character without any Melee Attack skills may still make unarmed attacks.

**Unarmed attacks** deal damage equal to the attacker's flat Strength bonus, minimum 1 point. No weapon is required, and no base die is rolled. Unarmed attacks do not benefit from Melee Attack skill bonuses.

**Untrained weapon use:** A character who attempts to use a melee weapon of a size they have not unlocked suffers a −4 penalty to the attack Opposition Check. Damage is unaffected.

## Training Tier

### Melee Attack I

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** None  
**Effect:** The character may use light melee weapons.

### Melee Attack II

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Melee Attack I  
**Effect:** +2 to melee attack rolls with light weapons.

### Melee Attack III

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Melee Attack II  
**Effect:** +2 to melee damage rolls with light weapons.

### Melee Attack IV

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Melee Attack III  
**Effect:** The character may use medium melee weapons.

### Melee Attack V

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Melee Attack IV  
**Effect:** +2 to melee attack rolls and melee damage rolls with medium weapons.

## Mastery Tier

### Melee Attack VI

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Melee Attack V  
**Effect:** The character may use heavy melee weapons. +1 AP (maximum 5 AP per turn).

### Melee Attack VII

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Melee Attack VI  
**Effect:** +2 to melee attack rolls and melee damage rolls with heavy weapons.

### Melee Attack VIII

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Melee Attack VII  
**Effect:** +1d10 to all melee attack rolls.

### Melee Attack IX

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Melee Attack VIII  
**Effect:** +1 additional damage die to all melee damage rolls.

### Melee Attack X

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Melee Attack IX  
**Effect:** +1 additional damage die to all melee damage rolls. +1 AP (maximum 5 AP per turn).
