# Combat

**Tree:** Universal

## Description

Combat covers all forms of direct physical and supernatural conflict. It represents a character's training in attacking and defending — whether with weapons, at range, through magic, or through disciplined defensive technique. Combat skills make characters more effective in encounters. The Combat node also includes the three armor skill trees (Light Armor, Medium Armor, Heavy Armor), which govern proficiency with and mastery of each armor category.

## Unarmed Attacks

1. Any character may make an unarmed attack without skills.
2. An unarmed attack is a standard melee attack.
3. Unarmed attacks deal damage equal to the attacker's flat Strength bonus, with a minimum of 1 point. There is no base weapon die.
