# Light Armor

**Branch:** Combat  
**Tree:** Universal  
**Prerequisites:** None

## Description

Light armor — padded, leather, studded leather — is the simplest protection available. It demands little strength to wear and does not restrict movement. Anyone can don a leather jerkin and move freely; training simply teaches the wearer to use it to its fullest potential, positioning the armor to intercept blows rather than treating it as little more than thickened clothing.

## Notes

1. Wearing light armor without Light Armor training uses the Mitigation (untrained) value from the armor table. No additional penalties apply — light armor is genuinely wearable without formal training.
2. Light Armor I grants access to the Mitigation (trained) value.
3. The +2 mastery bonus (across Light Armor II and III) is cumulative with the trained mitigation value.

## Skills

### Light Armor I

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** None  
**Effect:** Trained mitigation when wearing light armor. Use the Mitigation (trained) column from the [armor table](../../../Equipment/Armor.md#armor-types) instead of Mitigation (untrained).

### Light Armor II

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Light Armor I  
**Effect:** +1 to damage mitigation when wearing light armor.

### Light Armor III

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Light Armor II  
**Effect:** +1 to damage mitigation when wearing light armor (total +2 from this tree).
