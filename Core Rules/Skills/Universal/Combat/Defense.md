# Defense

**Branch:** Combat  
**Tree:** Universal  
**Prerequisites:** None

## Description

Defense represents a character's training in avoiding and deflecting attacks. It covers the three core defensive techniques — Dodge, Parry, and Block — improving the character's ability to respond to incoming attacks. Early investment improves Reflex broadly, benefiting all defensive rolls. Mastery allows the character to specialize in a preferred defensive technique.

## Notes

1. Defense IV and Defense V each require the player to choose one defensive skill: Dodge, Parry, or Block.
2. The choice at IV and V are independent. A player may choose the same defensive skill twice or two different defensive skills.
3. The chosen defensive skill is recorded on the character sheet at the time of purchase.
