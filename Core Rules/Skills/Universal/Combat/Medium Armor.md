# Medium Armor

**Branch:** Combat  
**Tree:** Universal  
**Prerequisites:** None

## Description

Medium armor — hide, chain shirt, scale mail — provides meaningful protection at the cost of some noise and bulk. An untrained wearer can buckle on a chain shirt, but will clank and scrape through every attempt at stealth. Training teaches the wearer to move methodically, keep the plates from rattling, and carry the weight in a way that leaves room to fight.

## Notes

1. Wearing medium armor without Medium Armor training uses the Mitigation (untrained) value from the armor table, and imposes a −2 penalty to Stealth Opposition Checks.
2. Medium Armor I grants access to the Mitigation (trained) value.
3. The +2 mastery bonus (across Medium Armor III and IV) is cumulative with the trained mitigation value.

## Skills

### Medium Armor I

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** None  
**Effect:** Trained mitigation when wearing medium armor. Use the Mitigation (trained) column from the [armor table](../../../Equipment/Armor.md#armor-types) instead of Mitigation (untrained).

### Medium Armor II

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Medium Armor I  
**Effect:** Removes the −2 Stealth Opposition Check penalty for wearing medium armor.

### Medium Armor III

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Medium Armor II  
**Effect:** +1 to damage mitigation when wearing medium armor.

### Medium Armor IV

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Medium Armor III  
**Effect:** +1 to damage mitigation when wearing medium armor (total +2 from this tree).
