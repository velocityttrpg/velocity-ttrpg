# Ranged Attack

**Branch:** Combat  
**Tree:** Universal  
**Prerequisites:** None

## Description

Ranged Attack represents a character's training in fighting at a distance. It covers the use of ranged weapons — bows, crossbows, thrown weapons, and similar arms. Skills in this line unlock weapon size categories, improve attack rolls and damage rolls with ranged weapons, and grant additional AP at higher tiers.

**Untrained weapon use:** A character who attempts to use a ranged weapon of a size they have not unlocked suffers a −4 penalty to the attack Opposition Check. Damage is unaffected.

## Training Tier

### Ranged Attack I

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** None  
**Effect:** The character may use light ranged weapons.

### Ranged Attack II

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Ranged Attack I  
**Effect:** +2 to ranged attack rolls with light weapons.

### Ranged Attack III

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Ranged Attack II  
**Effect:** +2 to ranged damage rolls with light weapons.

### Ranged Attack IV

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Ranged Attack III  
**Effect:** The character may use medium ranged weapons.

### Ranged Attack V

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Ranged Attack IV  
**Effect:** +2 to ranged attack rolls and ranged damage rolls with medium weapons.

## Mastery Tier

### Ranged Attack VI

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Ranged Attack V  
**Effect:** The character may use heavy ranged weapons. +1 AP (maximum 5 AP per turn).

### Ranged Attack VII

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Ranged Attack VI  
**Effect:** +2 to ranged attack rolls and ranged damage rolls with heavy weapons.

### Ranged Attack VIII

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Ranged Attack VII  
**Effect:** +1d10 to all ranged attack rolls.

### Ranged Attack IX

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Ranged Attack VIII  
**Effect:** +1 additional damage die to all ranged damage rolls.

### Ranged Attack X

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Ranged Attack IX  
**Effect:** +1 additional damage die to all ranged damage rolls. +1 AP (maximum 5 AP per turn).
