# Heavy Armor

**Branch:** Combat  
**Tree:** Universal  
**Prerequisites:** None

## Description

Heavy armor — chain mail, splint, plate — is the highest protection available and the most demanding discipline to master. An untrained wearer can put it on; they cannot fight effectively in it. The weight impedes reaction time. The noise makes stealth impossible. The mass cuts movement. Each skill in this tree marks a training threshold: a point where the armor stops being an obstacle and becomes part of the warrior.

## Notes

1. Wearing heavy armor without Heavy Armor training uses the Mitigation (untrained) value from the armor table, and imposes the following penalties: −2 to Reflex-based Opposition Checks, −5 ft per AP spent on movement, and −4 to Stealth Opposition Checks.
2. Heavy Armor I grants access to the Mitigation (trained) value.
3. The +2 mastery bonus (across Heavy Armor V and VI) is cumulative with the trained mitigation value.

## Skills

### Heavy Armor I

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** None  
**Effect:** Trained mitigation when wearing heavy armor. Use the Mitigation (trained) column from the [armor table](../../../Equipment/Armor.md#armor-types) instead of Mitigation (untrained).

### Heavy Armor II

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Heavy Armor I  
**Effect:** Removes the −2 Reflex penalty for wearing heavy armor.

### Heavy Armor III

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Heavy Armor II  
**Effect:** Removes the −5 ft per AP movement penalty for wearing heavy armor.

### Heavy Armor IV

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Heavy Armor III  
**Effect:** Removes the −4 Stealth Opposition Check penalty for wearing heavy armor.

### Heavy Armor V

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Heavy Armor IV  
**Effect:** +1 to damage mitigation when wearing heavy armor.

### Heavy Armor VI

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Heavy Armor V  
**Effect:** +1 to damage mitigation when wearing heavy armor (total +2 from this tree).
