# Survival

**Tree:** Universal

## Description

Survival covers the practical skills that keep characters alive and functional under pressure — moving unseen, following trails, enduring punishment, navigating the wilderness, treating wounds, and maintaining peak physical capability. These are not glamorous skills. They are the ones that matter when everything else has gone wrong.

## Notes

1. Survival is a node, not a purchasable skill. No Skill Point purchase is required to access its branches.
2. Each branch is independent. A character may invest in any branch without purchasing others.
