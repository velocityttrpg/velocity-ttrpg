# Wilderness

**Branch:** Survival  
**Tree:** Universal  
**Prerequisites:** None

## Description

Wilderness covers the skills that keep a character alive and oriented outside of civilization — finding food and water, building shelter, reading weather, and navigating terrain without landmarks or tools. In a Veilborn campaign, urban characters eventually leave the city. When they do, Wilderness is what separates a difficult journey from a fatal one.

## Training Tier

### Wilderness I

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** None  
**Effect:** +1 to Wilderness Opposition Checks.

### Wilderness II

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Wilderness I  
**Effect:** +1 to Wilderness Opposition Checks.

### Wilderness III

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Wilderness II  
**Effect:** +1 to Wilderness Opposition Checks.

### Wilderness IV

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Wilderness III  
**Effect:** The character can always locate food and clean water in natural environments. No Opposition Check is required under normal conditions.

### Wilderness V

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Wilderness IV  
**Effect:** +1 to Wilderness Opposition Checks.
