# Perception

**Branch:** Survival  
**Tree:** Universal  
**Prerequisites:** None

## Description

Perception is the trained habit of reading a room — tracking exits, clocking faces, registering the thing that doesn't fit. Most people move through the world looking at what's in front of them. A character trained in Perception is always watching the edges. At mastery, the world cannot hide relevant information from them: they simply ask, and the Narrator must answer.
