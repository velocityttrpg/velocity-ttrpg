# Athletics

**Branch:** Survival  
**Tree:** Universal  
**Prerequisites:** None

## Description

Athletics represents raw physical capability — the strength, endurance, and technique required to push the body past ordinary limits. Investment in Athletics builds functional strength and expands a character's capacity in water and on vertical surfaces, turning obstacles into options.
