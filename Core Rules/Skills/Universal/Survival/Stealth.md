# Stealth

**Branch:** Survival  
**Tree:** Universal  
**Prerequisites:** None

## Description

Stealth is the discipline of moving through the world without being seen, heard, or noticed — tailing a mark through a crowded street, slipping past a guard, disappearing into an alley before a vampire locks onto your scent. Investment in Stealth makes a character harder to detect and harder to track. At mastery, they can vanish in plain sight.

## Training Tier

### Stealth I

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** None  
**Effect:** +1 to Stealth Opposition Checks.

### Stealth II

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Stealth I  
**Effect:** +1 to Stealth Opposition Checks.

### Stealth III

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Stealth II  
**Effect:** +1 to Stealth Opposition Checks.

### Stealth IV

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Stealth III  
**Effect:** While hidden, the character may take one action without automatically breaking stealth. Whether stealth is broken remains subject to an Opposition Check at the Narrator's discretion.

### Stealth V

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Stealth IV  
**Effect:** +1 to Stealth Opposition Checks.

### Stealth VI

### Stealth VII

### Stealth VIII

**Cost:** 1 SP

### Stealth IX

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Stealth VIII  
**Effect:** +1 to Stealth Opposition Checks.

### Stealth X

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Stealth IX  
**Effect:** Once per session, the character may hide in plain sight without cover or concealment. This ability does not require an Opposition Check to initiate, but maintaining stealth while observed remains subject to normal checks.
