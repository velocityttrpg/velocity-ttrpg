# Apothecary

**Branch:** Survival  
**Tree:** Universal  
**Prerequisites:** None

## Description

Apothecary is the trained ability to keep people alive under adverse conditions. At its foundation it is improvised — tourniquets, bandaging, surgery with whatever is on hand. A character who invests deeply enough unlocks proper medical equipment: first the Healer's Bag, then the Master Healer's Satchel. Along the way they accumulate a toolkit of improvised techniques that remain useful long after a kit runs dry.

## Notes

1. **Improvised materials** — Techniques at tiers I–IV require scavenged or purchased materials (cloth, thread, needle, herbs, alcohol, etc.). A Wilderness check can source these in the field; otherwise they must be purchased.
2. **Healer's Bag** — 10 charges. Contains bandages, healing salves, herbal remedies, and basic tools. Each charge restores 1d6 + the apothecary's Willpower in Health. Unlocked at Apothecary V.
3. **Master Healer's Satchel** — 10 charges. Contains alchemical antidotes, restorative tinctures, stimulant compounds, and coagulants. Each charge restores 2d6 + the apothecary's Willpower in Health. Unlocked at Apothecary VII. A master apothecary may also use a Healer's Bag if no Satchel is available.
4. Both items must be purchased and carried. Each use depletes one charge. An empty item must be replaced or restocked.

## Training Tier

### Apothecary I

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** None  
**Effect:** +1 to Apothecary Opposition Checks. The character can apply a tourniquet to a Wounded or Dying character outside combat, halting Bleeding Out without fully stabilizing them. The character remains Wounded or Dying but no longer loses Health from bleeding.

### Apothecary II

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Apothecary I  
**Effect:** +1 to Apothecary Opposition Checks. The character can use improvised bandaging to restore 1d4 Health to a character outside combat. Requires scavenged or purchased materials. Takes 10 minutes. The character can perform Forced Awakening — see [Stabilization](../../../Characters/Stabilization.md) §6.

### Apothecary III

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Apothecary II  
**Effect:** The character can manage pain and minor conditions using herbs, alcohol, or improvised analgesics. Removes the Hindered condition and other pain-based conditions from a character. Takes one scene.

### Apothecary IV

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Apothecary III  
**Effect:** The character can perform minor surgery — sutures, wound closure, tooth extractions, and splinting — outside combat. Surgery closes wounds against future bleed risk and removes structural conditions. After surgery, the patient makes a Fortitude Opposition Check against a resistance factor set by the Narrator based on wound severity; on a success, the patient recovers 1d4 Health. Surgery does not directly restore Health and does not substitute for a Healer's Bag. Requires improvised tools (needle, thread, alcohol for sterilization).

### Apothecary V

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Apothecary IV  
**Effect:** The character can use a Healer's Bag to restore Health, using up to 2 charges per treatment.

## Mastery Tier

### Apothecary VI

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Apothecary V  
**Effect:** +1 to Apothecary Opposition Checks. The character can use up to 3 Healer's Bag charges per treatment.

### Apothecary VII

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Apothecary VI  
**Effect:** The character can use a Master Healer's Satchel, using up to 2 charges per treatment.

### Apothecary VIII

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Apothecary VII  
**Effect:** +1d10 to Apothecary Opposition Checks. The character can use up to 3 Master Healer's Satchel charges per treatment.

### Apothecary IX

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Apothecary VIII  
**Effect:** The character can use up to 4 Master Healer's Satchel charges per treatment. The character can fully neutralize a poison using 1 Master Healer's Satchel charge — the satchel's antidotes and alchemical compounds address the specific toxin. Does not require an improvised substitute.

### Apothecary X

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** Apothecary IX  
**Effect:** +1d10 to Apothecary Opposition Checks. The character can use up to 5 Master Healer's Satchel charges per treatment. The character can attempt to revive a recently deceased character — the body must be intact and death must be recent. Requires a Master Healer's Satchel.
