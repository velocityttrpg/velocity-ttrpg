# Tracking

**Branch:** Survival  
**Tree:** Universal  
**Prerequisites:** None

## Description

Tracking is the ability to read the evidence of passage — footprints, disturbed surfaces, scent trails, and the hundred small signs that a person or creature leaves behind without knowing it. In an urban environment, it becomes pattern recognition: familiar routes, predictable behaviors, the trail of a person moving through the city. At mastery, a tracker can follow someone who knew they were being followed.
