# Empathy

**Branch:** Attributes
**Tree:** Universal
**Prerequisites:** None

## Description

Empathy represents emotional intelligence and the capacity to read, connect with, and influence others through understanding. It is one of the two branches that build the Social attribute (alongside Dominance); both grant identical +Social bonuses, differing in flavor and capstone effects. Empathy is the flat bonus on Persuasion checks and Empathy-based Opposition Checks. A character with high Empathy doesn't manipulate — they genuinely resonate. Investment in the Empathy branch makes a character a powerful social force through warmth, insight, and emotional precision rather than authority or threat.
