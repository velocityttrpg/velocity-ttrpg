# Strength

**Branch:** Attributes  
**Tree:** Universal  
**Prerequisites:** None

## Description

Strength represents raw physical power — the capacity to hit hard, lift heavy, and overpower opposition. It is the flat bonus on melee and ranged attack rolls and on damage rolls for both. Investment in the Strength branch makes a character physically formidable: harder to resist, harder to ignore, and capable of feats of strength that ordinary people cannot match.
