# Dominance

**Branch:** Attributes
**Tree:** Universal
**Prerequisites:** None

## Description

Dominance represents force of will expressed outward — the capacity to impose presence, authority, and intent on others. It is one of the two branches that build the Social attribute (alongside Empathy); both grant identical +Social bonuses, differing in flavor and capstone effects. Dominance is the flat bonus on Intimidation checks and Domination-based Opposition Checks. A character with high Dominance doesn't need to raise their voice. They fill a room. Investment in the Dominance branch makes a character socially commanding and increasingly difficult to resist through authority alone.
