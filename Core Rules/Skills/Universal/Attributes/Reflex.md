# Reflex

**Branch:** Attributes  
**Tree:** Universal  
**Prerequisites:** None

## Description

Reflex represents physical quickness and reactive instinct — the body's capacity to respond to threats before the mind fully processes them. It is the flat bonus on all defensive rolls: Dodge, Parry, and Block. Investment in the Reflex branch makes a character harder to hit across all defensive techniques, and at mastery converts a successful defense into an immediate burst of action economy.
