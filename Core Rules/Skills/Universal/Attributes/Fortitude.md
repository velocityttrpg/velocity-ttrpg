# Fortitude

**Branch:** Attributes  
**Tree:** Universal  
**Prerequisites:** None

## Description

Fortitude is the body's trained resistance to environmental punishment — poison, disease, exhaustion, and extremes of heat and cold. A character trained in Fortitude doesn't just survive hardship; they endure through it. At mastery, they shrug off toxins that would kill a lesser constitution, resist the worst conditions any environment can produce, and carry more punishment before environmental threats take hold.
