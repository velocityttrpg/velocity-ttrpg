# Willpower

**Branch:** Attributes  
**Tree:** Universal  
**Prerequisites:** None

## Description

Willpower represents mental fortitude, supernatural potency, and the strength of a character's inner resolve. It is the flat bonus on magical attack rolls, spell damage, and Concentration checks. A character with high Willpower channels more force into every working and holds their focus under pressure that would break lesser minds. At mastery, they can resist domination, compulsion, and fear that would shatter anyone else.
