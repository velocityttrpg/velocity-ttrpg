# Vitality

**Branch:** Attributes  
**Tree:** Universal  
**Prerequisites:** None

## Description

Vitality represents physical endurance, biological resilience, and the capacity to absorb punishment and keep going. It determines a character's maximum Health. All characters begin with a base Vitality of 25. Investment in the Vitality branch increases that ceiling, making the character harder to bring down. Lineage skills can push Vitality beyond what this tree can achieve, reflecting the supernatural hardiness of non-human beings.

Each skill in this tree costs Skill Points equal to its tier number — Vitality I costs 1 SP, Vitality II costs 2 SP, and so on up to Vitality X at 10 SP. The full tree costs 55 SP and raises maximum Vitality to 65.

## Notes

1. Vitality determines a character's maximum Health. Increasing Vitality increases maximum Health by the same amount.
2. Lineage skills may increase Vitality beyond the values achievable through this tree.
