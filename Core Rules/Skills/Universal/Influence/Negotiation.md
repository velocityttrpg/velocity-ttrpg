# Negotiation

**Branch:** Influence  
**Tree:** Universal  
**Prerequisites:** Influence I  
**Attribute:** Social

## Description

Negotiation represents a character's ability to reach outcomes through reason, compromise, and reading what the other party wants. It covers transactional dealmaking, information extraction from reluctant sources, and any situation where the goal is agreement or disclosure rather than emotional influence or raw dominance. A skilled negotiator adapts their approach — warm or authoritative — to whatever the moment demands. At mastery, even a failed negotiation can be salvaged.

## Notes

1. Skills that improve an attribute (IV, V, VIII) grant +1 Social.
2. Negotiation Opposition Checks use Social as the flat attribute bonus.
