# Persuasion

**Branch:** Influence  
**Tree:** Universal  
**Prerequisites:** Influence I  
**Attribute:** Social

## Description

Persuasion represents a character's ability to move others through emotional appeal, shared values, and empathic connection. It is the art of making someone want to agree — not through fear or transaction, but through genuine resonance. A skilled persuader reads what people need and speaks directly to it. At mastery, a persuader can shift the feelings of a crowd as easily as an individual.
