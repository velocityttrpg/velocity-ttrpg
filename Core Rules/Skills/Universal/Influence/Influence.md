# Influence

**Tree:** Universal

## Description

Influence represents a character's ability to affect others through force of personality. It is the foundation of all social combat — the capacity to move people, unsettle them, or bring them to the table. Influence does not specify how the character achieves this effect; that is determined by which branch they pursue. Investment in the Influence trunk makes a character broadly more effective in any social contest before they specialize.

## Trunk

### Influence I

**Cost:** 1 SP  
**Prerequisite:** None  
**Effect:** +1 to all Influence Opposition Checks (Persuasion, Intimidation, and Negotiation). Unlocks the Persuasion, Intimidation, and Negotiation branches.
