# Inventory

## Inventory slots

1. A character's inventory capacity is measured in slots, not weight or coin value.
2. All characters begin with 50 inventory slots.
3. A character gains 3 additional slots per point of Strength.
4. Total inventory capacity is recalculated whenever a character's Strength changes.

## Item sizes

1. Every item has a size: Tiny, Small, Medium, or Large.
2. Tiny and Small items each consume 1 slot.
3. Medium items consume 2 slots.
4. Large items consume 3 slots.
5. A character can't carry an item larger than Large.

## Stacking

1. Tiny and Small items of the same type stack together in a single slot.
2. A stack of Tiny or Small items of the same type consumes 1 slot regardless of quantity.
3. Medium and Large items don't stack; each instance consumes its own slots.

## Capacity

1. A character can't carry items whose total slot cost exceeds their inventory capacity.
2. If a character's inventory capacity decreases below their current slot usage (e.g., from a temporary Strength reduction), they aren't forced to drop items immediately, but can't pick up additional items until they're under capacity again.
