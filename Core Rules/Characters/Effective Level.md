# Effective level

## Definition

1. Effective level (EL) is a numerical measure of total character capability.  
2. Effective level is equal to the number of spent skill points a character has invested in skills and attribute nodes.  
3. Effective level is used for character creation, NPC creation, enemy creation, and as the basis for group effective level.

## Calculating effective level

1. A character’s effective level is equal to their total spent skill points.  
2. Unspent skill points do not contribute to effective level.

## Effective level and characters

1. Effective level represents the total capability a character has acquired through skills and attribute growth.  
2. Characters with higher effective level have invested more skill points and possess greater breadth or depth of abilities.

## Effective level and non‑player characters

1. NPCs use the same effective level calculation as player characters.  
2. To create an NPC using skill points:

    1. Assign a number of skill points equal to the desired effective level.  
    2. Purchase skills and attribute nodes as desired.  
    3. Record the resulting abilities and modifiers.

3. For monsters ported from d20-based bestiaries (Challenge Rating stat blocks), see Porting d20 Monsters for converting CR directly to a skill point budget.

## Base effective level

1. Base effective level represents the average capability of a group before group size is considered.  
2. Base effective level is calculated as the average effective level of all members of the group, rounded up to the nearest whole number.

## Group effective level

1. Group effective level represents the combined capability of a group of characters or enemies.  
2. Group effective level accounts for both the base effective level of the group and the number of members in the group.  
3. Group effective level is determined by:

    1. Determining the group size.  
    2. Determining the base effective level of the group.  
    3. Using the [Party Effective Level Calculation table](../Tables/Party%20Effective%20Level%20Calculation.md) to look up the group effective level based on group size and base effective level.

4. The Party Effective Level Calculation table provides the final group effective level for all combinations of group size (1–20) and base effective level (1–20).  
5. No mathematical calculation is required at the table; narrators and players use the lookup table directly.
