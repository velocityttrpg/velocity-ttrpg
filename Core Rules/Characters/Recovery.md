# Recovery

## Recovery Dice

1. Natural recovery uses fixed values: 1 Health per four hours of nonstrenuous activity, 1d4 for a light rest, 2d4 for a long rest.
2. A lineage may specify different recovery values, replacing these defaults.

## Natural Recovery

1. A character recovers 1 Health for every four hours of nonstrenuous activity.
2. A light rest of less than six hours restores 1d4 Health.
3. A long rest of six or more hours restores 2d4 Health.
4. Natural recovery cannot increase a character’s Health above their Vitality.
5. Natural recovery is halted during combat.
6. Natural recovery is halted during periods of strenuous activity.
7. Natural recovery is halted while the character is dying.

## Medical Recovery

1. A stabilized character may receive medical treatment to recover Health.
2. Medical recovery requires appropriate medical tools.
3. Medical recovery requires Apothecary V or higher (the tier that grants Healer's Bag use).
4. Medical recovery restores Health using a Healer's Bag (1d6 + Willpower per charge) or, for a character with Apothecary VII or higher, a Master Healer's Satchel (2d6 + Willpower per charge), up to the number of charges per treatment permitted by the healer's Apothecary tier.
5. Medical recovery cannot be performed during combat.

## Supernatural Recovery

1. A character may recover Health through supernatural means if such methods exist in the setting.
2. Supernatural recovery restores Health immediately.
3. Supernatural recovery may have additional effects depending on the method used.

## Unconsciousness

1. A stabilized character who is unconscious remains unconscious until a healer removes the Unconscious condition from them (see [Stabilization](Stabilization.md) §2.3 and §5.2), or until forcibly awakened (see [Stabilization](Stabilization.md) §6).
2. A forcibly awakened character retains all conditions present at the time of awakening except Unconscious. See [Stabilization](Stabilization.md) §6.
3. A character who regains consciousness naturally may act normally unless affected by other conditions.

## Restrictions on Recovery

1. A character cannot recover Health while dying.
2. A character cannot recover Health while dead.
3. A character cannot recover Health through more than one method in the same recovery action — only one of natural, medical, or supernatural recovery may apply at a time.
4. A character cannot exceed their Vitality through recovery.
