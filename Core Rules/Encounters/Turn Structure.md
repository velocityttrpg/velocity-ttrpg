# Turn Structure

## Turn Structure

1. Each participant receives 3 Action Points (AP) at the start of their turn.  
2. Additional AP may be granted by lineage, skills, or effects.  
3. AP represent the total effort, focus, and exertion the participant can commit during their turn.  
4. AP refresh at the start of each of the participant’s turns.  
5. If the participant is maintaining a Concentration effect with an AP cost, that cost must be paid from this turn's AP before it is spent on anything else (see [Concentration](Concentration.md) §2).  

## Using Actions

1. A participant may spend AP in any order during their turn.  
2. Actions cost AP as listed in their descriptions.  
3. Unspent AP don’t carry over to the next turn.  
4. A participant may interleave movement with actions as long as they have AP and movement available.  

## Action Types

1. Free Actions don’t cost AP unless a rule states otherwise.  
2. Martial Actions cost AP as listed in their descriptions.  
3. Supernatural Actions cost AP as listed in their descriptions.  
4. Reactions may be taken outside the participant’s turn when their trigger occurs and cost AP as listed.  
5. Movement is purchased with AP and follows the rules in the Movement chapter.  

## Ending The Turn

1. A participant’s turn ends when all AP have been spent or when the participant voluntarily ends their turn.  
2. Any unspent AP are lost at the end of the turn.  
3. Effects that last “until the start of your next turn” or “until the end of your turn” resolve at this point.
