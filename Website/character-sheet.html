Velocity

Character Sheet · Veilborn / Anar

Archetype

Archetypes are starting templates only — Velocity has no classes, and every skill point spent here can be refunded and reallocated freely as the character grows. Selecting an archetype spends Skill Points on a suggested starting build and adds matching starter gear to the Inventory; switching or clearing the archetype refunds those skills and removes that gear. "Reset Character" clears the entire sheet back to its default blank state.

All attributes begin at 0 (Vitality begins at 50). 2 points assigned at creation (max 3 each before lineage). No lineage available at creation modifies core attributes. Skills that grant attribute bonuses (e.g. Attributes tree, Defense, Athletics, Persuasion, Intimidation) add directly to the value shown here — editing the field by hand adjusts your base score independent of those skill bonuses. Other effects granted by purchased Attribute-tree skills (bonus dice, Opposition Check bonuses, etc.) are listed below each attribute in gold. Death occurs at 0 Health. While at or below the dying threshold, make Consciousness Checks (dice pool + higher of Willpower or Fortitude) per Dying.md. Bleeding out: 1% of Vitality (min 1) per round at end of turn until stabilized. Each character gets 3 AP per turn (see Encounters/Actions.md); unspent AP do not carry over.

Weapons & Damage

Melee/Ranged Damage = weapon base die, plus one bonus die of the same size per 3 points of Strength, plus any stacking flat-d6 bonuses from skills (Strength VII/IX, Melee/Ranged Attack X). Magic Damage = the spell's own base die (set here to match the spell being cast) plus one bonus die of the same size per 3 points of Willpower, plus stacking d6 bonuses from Willpower VII/IX and Magic Attack IV/IX.

Armor & Mitigation

Damage Mitigation is a flat reduction applied to each hit, per Equipment/Armor.md §7 (Light 2 / Medium 4 / Heavy 6).

Bottomless Coinpurse

10 cp = 1 sp, 10 sp = 1 gp, 10 gp = 1 pp. The Bottomless Coinpurse is a Bag of Holding for wealth — coins, gems, and jewels stored here have no weight and take no inventory slots. If lost or destroyed, loose wealth occupies slots (1 per denomination; gems by type). A replacement costs 50 gp.

Skill Points

Skills purchased below: 0 SP (1 SP per checked skill). Checking a skill automatically deducts 1 from Unspent SP and adds 1 to Spent SP; unchecking refunds it. Skills must be purchased in order — a skill is locked until its predecessor in that line is purchased.

Skills

Check each purchased skill (skills are purchased in order; a skill's prerequisite is the skill before it in its line, unless noted otherwise). Hover a skill for its effect. See the skill constellation diagrams (Tools/Skill Trees/skill-trees.html) for full details.
