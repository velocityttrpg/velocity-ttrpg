## Second Review — 2026-06-29

Eight issues identified across Critical, Significant, and Minor categories. All eight resolved.

### ~~R2-1. Sela's Magic Attack skill table wrong~~ — RESOLVED 2026-06-29
Magic Attack I description incorrectly said "+1 to Magic Attack Opposition Checks." Core Rules: tier I grants Level 0 spell access only, no bonus. Tier II grants +2 to attack rolls; tier III grants +2 to magic damage rolls. Tier III's damage bonus was also missing from the table. Fixed: skill table corrected for all three tiers; "+2 to magic damage rolls" added to tier III; magic damage line added to Key Rolls.

### ~~R2-2. Kalen stag damage used flat Strength bonus (doesn't exist)~~ — RESOLVED 2026-06-29
"1d12 + 3" and "1d8 + 3" applied Strength as a flat damage bonus. Core Rules: Strength gives a flat bonus to *attack rolls* only; damage bonus is 1 bonus die per 3 points. Mike's call: antlers set to 2d8 + 4, hooves to 2d6 + 4 (Velocity-native values, stag does not appear in d20 SRD).

### ~~R2-3. The Docks attributed Liriel's imprisonment to Reth~~ — RESOLVED 2026-06-29
Established canon: Reth removed the Truthstone at Voss's direction; Voss personally arranged Liriel's imprisonment. `Locations/The Docks.md` incorrectly credited Reth with all three actions. Corrected.

### ~~R2-4. Guard continuity — same two guards at Counting House and tribunal hall~~ — RESOLVED 2026-06-29
Voss's Guards NPC file had two guards; they appeared in both Act 2 (Counting House) and Act 3 (tribunal hall). Resolved: Act 3 fight now specifies six to eight guards (one to two per player) — a separate contingent stationed at the tribunal hall. NPC file header updated. The Counting House retains its two guards independently.

### ~~R2-5. Tribunal Hall location file missing Aldric Fenn~~ — RESOLVED 2026-06-29
Fenn is a named magistrate on the dais with his own NPC file. Added to `Locations/The Tribunal Hall.md` Key NPCs list.

### ~~R2-6. Falcon attack roll: wrong attribute, misapplied bonus~~ — RESOLVED 2026-06-29
Attack roll used Reflex (defense attribute) and added Keen Senses +4 (a perception bonus). Mike's call: falcon uses Reflex for attacks as a monster exception — speed and precision, not mass. Keen Senses removed from the attack roll. Note added to stat block documenting the exception.

### ~~R2-7. Counting House subcellar: incoherent Truthstone note~~ — RESOLVED 2026-06-29
"A Faerie who touches it can use Sense Truth immediately" — Faeries have Sense Truth inherently; they don't need the stone. Sentence removed.

### ~~R2-8. Overview synopsis: ambiguous phrasing~~ — RESOLVED 2026-06-29
"The Truthstone she left in her absence" implied Liriel intentionally left the stone as a substitute. Simplified to "So is the Truthstone."
