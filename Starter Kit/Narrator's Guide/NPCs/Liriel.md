# Liriel
*The Truthteller*

## Stat Block

**Lineage:** Faerie  
**Vitality:** 25 | **Health:** 25 (weakened — treat as 15 when found) | **Dying threshold:** 13

Liriel is unarmed and cannot fight. She has no relevant combat skills.

**Sense Truth** (Faerie trait, always active): Liriel cannot be lied to directly. A direct false statement registers immediately. Within 10 feet of a speaker, she knows whether what they are saying is true.

---

## What She Is

A Faerie who has served Dunmore's tribunal as Truthteller for many years. Older, well-regarded, deeply respected by the town's older residents. Her absence from the tribunal is not merely irregular — to people who know her, it is alarming.

## What She Knows

She was taken before she could attend the tribunal. The men who came for her were hired muscle with no obvious affiliation — she cannot identify who sent them. She knows the Truthstone is in the same room she is being held in. She knows what was done to her is illegal.

## What She Wants

To live. To testify.

## How She Plays

Calm despite her circumstances. She is old enough to have survived worse, and she has a Faerie's particular relationship with patience. If reached in time she is immediately cooperative — she has no agenda beyond telling the truth, which is the only thing she has ever done.

Faeries cannot lie. This is not reputation — it is what they are. Her testimony needs no stone, no oath, no corroboration. When she speaks, the hall knows it is true. If Voss attempts to deny her account and she is within ten feet of him, she will say so plainly: *"That is a lie."* She cannot do otherwise. She will not soften it.
