# Seren Holt
*The chief advocate*

## Stat Block

**Vitality:** 25 | **Health:** 25 | **Wounded threshold:** 13 | **Dying threshold:** 7

| Attribute | Score |
|-----------|------:|
| Social | 1 |

**Skills:** Influence I, Intimidation II, Negotiation I.  
**Armor:** None  
**Weapon:** None

Holt does not fight. She is a legal official, not a combatant. Physically threatening her ends the adventure badly for the players — she is the institution, not the conspiracy.

---

## What She Is

The tribunal's appointed prosecutor. Cold, procedural, and hungry for a verdict.

## What She Knows

Less than the players might assume. Holt is not part of Voss's conspiracy. She genuinely believes Kalen is guilty — the charges were presented to her with enough supporting documentation to satisfy her standards, which are not high when the accused is someone she already dislikes.

## What She Wants

A conviction, on record, by end of day.

## How She Plays

The obvious villain in Act 1. She dismisses the players' testimony, presses the tribunal forward, and treats the missing Truthstone as an administrative inconvenience. She is unpleasant but not corrupt. Investigating her is a dead end — and a good use of time the players don't have.

When Liriel walks into the tribunal hall in Act 3, Holt does not fight the outcome. She is a zealot for process, and the process has spoken. She formally withdraws the charges. She does not apologize. She does not look at Voss.
