# The Docks

## Description

Dunmore sits on a river — not wide enough for ocean vessels, but sufficient for barges and small trade boats moving goods between inland towns. The docks are working docks: rope, mud, the smell of fish and river water, crates stacked under oilcloth.

Two vessels are currently moored. One departs tomorrow morning for Holwick. The other leaves the day after for points south.

## Key NPCs Present

- **Davan Reth** — if players have not yet found and confronted him elsewhere, he is here, watching the boats and waiting for word that the contract has been signed

## Finding Reth at the Docks

If the Broken Wheel regulars mentioned that Reth was asking about departing ships, players may come here directly. A Tracking check (difficulty 11 — easier than the trail to his room, since the docks are a natural destination) confirms he has been here recently and is likely still nearby.

Reth is not hiding. He does not yet believe he needs to hide. He has a drink in hand, he is watching the river, and he looks like a man waiting for a courier.

## Confronting Reth Here

The docks give Reth options — he knows the layout, and the river is a possible escape route. If combat or a chase begins, he will move toward the moored boats rather than into town.

A successful Intimidation check (difficulty 13) or physical restraint stops him before he gets that far. If he reaches the water, an Athletics or Movement check (difficulty 14) is needed to cut him off before he can call for help from the boat crew — who are not combatants but could complicate the situation.

Once restrained or sufficiently intimidated, Reth cooperates. He is not loyal to Holwick. He is loyal to getting paid and getting out. With neither of those available, he will trade information for whatever terms the players can offer him.

## What Reth Will Say

If players have already found the letter and the list, Reth knows they know enough to hang him. He will confirm:

- The Holwick Spice Company's hidden operations — narcotics and slavery, the Karrath waystation, the scope of the operation
- That Voss knew what Holwick was before the deal was finalised
- That he arranged the charges against Kalen and the removal of the Truthstone at Voss's direction — Voss personally arranged the men holding Liriel
- That the two crossed-out names on his list are dead — "resolved" the way Kalen was almost resolved

He will not volunteer this last detail. A player who asks directly about the crossed-out names will get an answer. A player who doesn't ask will not.
