# Dunmore

## Overview

Dunmore is a mid-sized market town on a well-traveled trade road, prosperous enough to have a formal tribunal hall and a chartered merchant guild, small enough that everyone knows Kalen Thorne. It has the self-importance of a town that has never had cause to doubt itself — until now.

The town sits on a river navigable by barge and small trade boat. It has a working dock, a market square, and the kind of civic infrastructure that comes from two generations of comfortable commerce. The Voss Trading Company is its most successful export business and a source of quiet civic pride.

## Atmosphere

Dunmore feels like a place where things are supposed to make sense. There are rules. There are customs. There is a tribunal hall with a carved seal above the door. The fact that those rules are being openly violated — in that hall, today — is what makes the town so unsettled.

When the players move through Dunmore during the investigation, the town is quietly frightened. People have noticed the Belar banking delegation. People know the Truthteller is missing. Nobody is saying much about either.

The unease has a second layer that fewer people will name aloud: the man on trial is a Shapeshifter. That makes it easier for some to stay quiet. It should not, and most of them know it.
