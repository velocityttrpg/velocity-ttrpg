# The Tribunal Hall

## Description

A stone building at the center of town, older than most of the structures around it. The carved seal above the door reads: *Truth Before All*. The irony is not lost on anyone.

Inside: vaulted ceilings that swallow sound and shadow in equal measure, heavy drapes drawn against the daylight, and a raised dais where the three magistrates sit. The room is designed to make the person standing at its center feel small. Today it is working.

The Truthstone's place on the advocate's table is empty. There is a ring of dust where it normally sits.

## Atmosphere

Crowded. Shoulder to shoulder. The whole town has come, drawn by the spectacle of a Shapeshifter in chains and a tribunal proceeding without its Truthteller. The whispers are uneasy rather than excited. People know something is wrong. They are not sure they want to be the one to say so.

## Key NPCs Present

- **Kalen Thorne** — chained at the center, beneath the shaft of light
- **Seren Holt** — at the advocate's table, gavel in hand
- **Aldric Voss** — on the dais, watching more carefully than the situation warrants
- **Aldric Fenn** — on the dais, the youngest magistrate, visibly uncomfortable
- Two unnamed tribunal guards flanking Kalen

## What Players Can Find Here

**The empty Truthstone space** (Investigation, difficulty 7 — auto-succeed for any character with Investigation flat bonus of 7+): The ring of dust shows the stone was removed recently and carefully. Not hurried. Someone who knew exactly what they were doing.

**Wax trace on the table** (Forensics, difficulty 11): A faint smear of sealing wax where the Truthstone sat. The same distinctive wax used to seal official VTC correspondence — identifiable if players later find VTC documents to compare it against.

**Voss watching the players** (Situational Awareness, difficulty 13): He is paying more attention to their reactions than to the proceedings. A magistrate concerned with justice watches the accused. Voss watches the witnesses.

## During the Recess

The hall empties but the guards remain. Kalen is moved to a holding room adjacent to the dais — a stone antechamber with a barred door. He can be reached through the bars. He cannot be reached by conventional means from outside the building without passing the guards.

He can, however, be reached by Mindspeech.
