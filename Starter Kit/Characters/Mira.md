# Mira

## What You Witnessed

You were at the Broken Wheel the night it happened. You were nursing a drink in the back, watching the room the way you always do, when the man sat down next to Kalen Thorne at the bar.

He leaned in close. Said something quiet — too quiet to carry. You couldn't hear it. But you watched Kalen's posture change: shoulders, jaw, hands. Whatever was said, it landed like a blade. And then Kalen hit him — hard, clean across the jaw, sent him off the stool and onto the floor — and walked out without looking back.

You've seen bar fights. This wasn't one. This was a man who had just been told something he couldn't let stand.

That's why you're in the tribunal hall. Not because you know what was said. Because you know what you saw.

## Playing Mira

Mira is the party's scout and ranged specialist. Stealth V means she can act — fire, move, signal — without breaking concealment once per round. Her +5 Stealth flat bonus auto-succeeds on most Routine and Challenging checks without a roll.

In the tribunal hall she is quiet. Her Intimidating Presence (+4) means she doesn't need Intimidation skills for the crossbow in the room to change a negotiation.

**Watch the flaws.** Against any mind-affecting ability (fear, charm, compulsion) she rolls at −2. Against poison, disease, or exhaustion she rolls at −4. These are real liabilities.
