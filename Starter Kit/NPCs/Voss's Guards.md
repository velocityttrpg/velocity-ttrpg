# Voss's Guards
*Two at the Counting House. Six to eight at the tribunal hall in Act 3.*

## Stat Block

**Vitality:** 25 | **Health:** 25 | **Dying threshold:** 13

| Attribute | Score |
|-----------|------:|
| Strength | 1 |

**Skills:** Melee Attack II (light weapons, +2 attack).  
**Armor:** Light (mitigation 3)  
**Weapon:** Shortsword (1d8)  
**Attack roll:** 1d10 + 3 (Melee Attack II +2, Strength +1)  
**3 AP per turn.**

---

## What They Are

Professional hired guards, not loyalists. They work for Voss because he pays well. They do not know what is in the subcellar.

## How They Play

They will hold their position until it becomes clear the situation is lost — at which point they reconsider. A successful Intimidation or Persuasion check (difficulty 13) makes them step aside without a fight. They will not die for Voss if the cause is clearly lost.

In Act 3, when Voss's conspiracy is exposed publicly, their willingness to act on his behalf evaporates entirely.
